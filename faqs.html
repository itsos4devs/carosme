How It Works

Step 1: Make an Appointment


It takes less than 2 minutes to submit your car and contact details on Carsome.my or call our hotline at 1800-82-3388. Our Customer Service team will call you to confirm your details and inspection date and time. Make an appointment here.


Step 2: Attend the Inspection


Attend the appointment with the original documents and items listed below:


a. Registration Card (RC)/Vehicle Ownership Certificate (VOC)

b. NRIC (or passport for non-Malaysians)

c. Spare keys (if available)

d. Approved Permit (AP) (company or individual for verification, if applicable)

e. Hire Purchase Settlement (for cars with outstanding loan amount)

f. Purchase Invoice (for cars purchased by cash)

g. Third-party authorization letter (for cars under a different name)


Step 3: Accept the Offer or Choose Online Bidding


We will quote you a selling price, which you may accept on the spot or choose to put your car up for online bidding.


A. Sell to CARSOME directly


Once you accept the offer, you will be given a maximum of 5 days to hand over your car and the necessary documents to us. If you agree to hand over your car on the day of inspection, the ownership transfer and payment process can be done on the same day if you bring all the necessary documents.


B. Put your car up for online bidding


We will schedule your car for bidding at the earliest available slot, where it will be bid by 2,300+ verified used car dealers. If your car receives bids, you have 24 hours to accept or reject the bid offer. If you accept, you will be given 5 days to prepare all the necessary documents and hand over the car to us.


Currently, CARSOME has 2 bidding sessions on Mondays to Saturdays (10am - 12pm and 2pm - 4pm); and 1 bidding session on Sundays (2pm - 4pm).


Step 4: Transfer of Ownership, Vehicle Handover, and Payment


All paperwork for ownership transfer will be handled by us on the agreed date of the vehicle handover. The remaining loan settlement with the bank and final payment will be made directly to the owner’s bank account via online transfer.

The inspection appointment takes an average of 30 - 45 minutes*. A CARSOME-certified auto inspector will conduct a thorough 175-point inspection of your car, which includes on-board diagnostics (OBD) scanning and a short road test. The road test helps us determine the realistic value of your car. Photos will also be taken for record and reporting purposes.


We will offer you a price to buy your car on the spot. If you agree to accept the offer and sell to us, we will complete all the necessary paperwork for you, and pay you via online transfer within 24 hours*. If you decide to try online bidding, we will schedule your car for a 2-hour online bidding among our verified used car dealer network. The starting bid price will be discussed and agreed upon between you and CARSOME prior to the live bidding session.
